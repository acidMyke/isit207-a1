About AZoom

Singapore's best electric car rental company self-proclaimed, confidently earned.

Who We Are

AZoom was built with a simple belief: renting a car in Singapore should be effortless, transparent, and future-ready.

We specialise exclusively in electric vehicles, offering a modern fleet that's clean, quiet, and perfectly suited for urban driving. No outdated cars. No confusing terms. Just a smooth experience from booking to drop-off.

Why Electric

Electric vehicles are the future and the present. They're better for the environment, better for the city, and better to drive.

At AZoom, every decision we make revolves around maximising efficiency, reducing emissions, and delivering a premium driving experience without the premium attitude.

Our Mission

To accelerate the adoption of electric vehicles by making them accessible, practical, and enjoyable for everyone.

Whether it's for a day, a week, or longer AZoom helps you move smarter.
